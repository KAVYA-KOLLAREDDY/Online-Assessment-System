Examination Guidelines

Please review all instructions before proceeding with your test.

  • The examination will take place in a secure, full-screen mode. Avoid minimizing, switching tabs, or exiting the screen.
  • Refrain from using browser navigation buttons, refreshing the page, or triggering keyboard shortcuts (e.g., F5, Alt+Tab, Ctrl+R).
  • Copying or capturing exam content is strictly prohibited and monitored.
  • Ensure your system is fully charged or connected to power throughout the exam.
  • A stable internet connection is required for the exam session. Interruption may result in submission.
  • The exam will auto-submit once the allotted time expires. Use your time wisely.
Important: Any suspicious or unauthorized action will result in immediate termination of your attempt and submission of your exam.
